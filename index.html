Shopify Business Partner Handbook

The role of a Business Partner at Shopify is to be the interface between Group Owners / Discipline Leaders and Talent issues. This means BPs need to know the ins and outs of their assigned groups/disciplines/regions and reduce as much friction as possible while continuing to focus on talent density and improvement of the efficiency + effectiveness of the discipline. They are not solely responsible for this (as this is jointly owned with Ops) but are singularly responsible as leaders of HR issues for the teams they support. Simply put: they are responsible for the push + pull of Shopify's Talent vision both to the company and to the Talent team.

  • Internal Moves Processes
  • Scope Changes
  • Impact & Mastery
  • Talent Reviews
  • Leaves
  • Development
  • Pulse
  • Compensation
  • Re-Orgs & RIFs
  • Exits

What we expect of BPs

  • Establish trust with GO / DLs + senior leadership team
  • Work with TA and DLs to be accountable for talent density - eg, help leaders with hiring prioritization, slots, team structure + any performance issues; they also help managers with performance issues + team structure
  • High domain expertise to navigate any HR issue and execute the fundamentals seamlessly (exits, ~mastery cycles, understanding the Talent roadmap)
  • Understanding Talent initiatives and be the responsible node to communicate and support those initiatives to their assigned Discipline Leader(s) and groups
  • Experts on Talent tooling and data literate
  • Represent their assigned Discipline point of view within Talent
