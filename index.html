Welcome to the Business Partner Handbook

This handbook serves as your comprehensive guide to navigating the Business Partner role at Shopify. Whether you're new to the role or looking to refresh your knowledge, you'll find essential information and resources here.

Use the navigation menu on the left to explore different aspects of your role, from internal processes to development opportunities.

Key Expectations

Strategic Partnership
Build and maintain strong relationships with leaders and teams to drive business outcomes.
Talent Development
Support the growth and development of talent through coaching, feedback, and development planning.
Data-Driven Insights
Leverage data and analytics to provide actionable insights and recommendations.
Change Management
Guide teams through organizational changes while maintaining engagement and productivity.
